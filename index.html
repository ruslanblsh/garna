How it works

From platform payout to your Garna balance

The flow is simple, but setup may differ by platform. During Early Access, we guide you through the important details.

1

Create your Garna account

Register in Garna and complete verification. KYC usually takes 1–2 business days.

2

Tell us your platform

Contact your payout manager and tell us where you receive income.

3

Get setup instructions

We’ll check the available payout method and provide the correct Garna details or next steps.

4

Request payout on the platform

Add the provided details where supported and request a withdrawal from your platform.

5

Create an invoice in Garna

Create an invoice and attach the required confirmation, such as a payout screenshot or platform document.

6

Receive and withdraw funds

Garna matches the payment with your invoice, credits your balance, and you withdraw using your preferred available method.

Platform processing time usually depends on the platform itself. In many cases, incoming funds may take 1–5 business days.
